.about

I am an Indonesian research assistant in Advanced Photonics and Photovoltaics (APP) group at The Center of Solar Energy Research and Applications (GUNAM) at Middle East Technical University (METU). I am now working towards my master of science degree in Micro and Nanotechnology program under supervision of Asst. Prof. Selcuk Yerci. I graduated from Physics department of METU in 2014 with high honour degree. My main research evolves around the fabrication of thin crystalline silicon solar cells with advanced light trapping. My side projects include thin silicon peeling with stressor layer and liquid epitaxy of germanium on insulator.

When I am not doing research, I enjoy tweaking my Android phone (LG G3). I often lurk around in XDA-Developers forum where you can get almost everything related to Android tweaking and development. I recently took up a hobby of web development, both designing and coding. This site is an example of my hobby that has turned into a live product.
